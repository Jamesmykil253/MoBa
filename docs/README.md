## Executive Summary

A dark comedy satirical technopunk MOBA with 3D platformer mechanics, designed for viral appeal and competitive depth. Matches range from 60 seconds to 10 minutes across 1v1, 3v3, and 5v5 configurations. Players battle as meme characters (starting with Elom Nusk and DOGE) to collect crypto coins and score points through strategic 3D combat and platformer movement.

**Detailed market positioning and competitive analysis available in [VISION.md](VISION.md)**

## Core Differentiators

1. **3D Platformer + MOBA Hybrid** - Jumping, double-jumping, and 3D movement add vertical strategy
2. **Meme Character Roster** - Satirical technopunk characters with viral potential  
3. **Multiple Match Lengths** - From 60-second micro-matches to 10-minute competitive games
4. **Crypto Coin Economy** - Players collect spinning crypto coins from kills/NPCs (no traditional orbs)
5. **Affordable Cosmetics** - $1-$5 pricing strategy vs competitors' $10-20+ pricing
6. **Cross-Platform Launch** - Mobile + PC day 1, console expansion planned
